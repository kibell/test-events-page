Campus Tours

Visiting campus is the perfect way to experience everything SNHU has to offer. Here, you can chat with an admission counselor, attend a brief information session and take a one-hour tour.

Campus tours are offered Monday through Friday at 9 am, 11:30 am and 2 pm.

Upcoming 2024 weekend tour dates include: Saturday, April 20, and Sunday, April 28. Weekend tours are at 11 am and 1:30 pm.

Upcoming 2024 Twilight Tour dates include: Thursday, March 28, Wednesday, April 17, Thursday, April 25, and Tuesday, April 30. Twilight Tours are at 4:30 pm.

Open Houses

This fall, we’re excited to offer four open house events to help students and families explore specific areas of study at SNHU:

  • Friday, October 27, 2023 – School of Engineering, Technology and Aeronautics
  • Sunday, November 5, 2023 – All Programs
  • Friday, November 17, 2023 – School of Business
  • Friday, December 1, 2023 – School of Arts, Sciences and Education

Our general open house on November 5 will provide information on all our academic programs, making it ideal for students who are undecided about their intended major.

All open houses will introduce students to life at SNHU and cover topics such as academics, involvement, residence life, community and more.

Accepted Student Days

PLEASE NOTE: Pre-registration for the March 23 Accepted Student Day is now closed. If you haven't pre-registered, you can register on site on the day of the event! Please arrive at the Athletic Complex (Building C on the campus map) at 9:00 am to register and check in. We look forward to seeing you!

Join the Office of Admission for an exciting day celebrating our accepted students and experience life as a Penman!

2024 Accepted Student Day dates include Saturday, March 23; Sunday, April 7, and Saturday, April 13. Events run from 9:00 am to 2:00 pm.

Each student to attend will receive a free long-sleeve tech shirt!

Students get to:

  • Attend academic sessions with faculty
  • Participate in student life activities
  • Have lunch and visit the Campus Resource Fair
  • Take a tour of our campus or residence halls

Breakout sessions for families and supporters include:

  • Public Safety & Residence Life
  • Academic Advising & Career Development
  • First Generation Learners
  • Diversity & Inclusivity

Families and supporters can also:

  • Make appointments with financial aid
  • Meet current students and staff
  • Explore our bookstore, esports arena and the Wolak Learning Center
